# Implementation Plan: Epic 5 - TAS Reporting & Executive Dashboard

## 1. Goal
Prioritize TAS operational visibility. Provide tools to track client trends, compare performance across periods (YoY/QoQ), and automate professional reporting.

## 2. Priority: TAS Operational Reporting (Story 5.5)

### Phase A: Trend & Comparison Engine
- **Logic**: Aggregation by Month/Quarter/Year.
- **Comparison**: 
    - **YoY**: Current Period vs. Same Period Last Year.
    - **QoQ**: Current Quarter vs. Previous Quarter.
- **Metrics**: Total Tickets, Total Actual Hours, Average Completion Time.

### Phase B: Interactive Dashboard
- **Visuals**: Line charts for trends, Bar charts for client distribution.
- **UX**: Dynamic filters for Client and Timeframe.

## 3. Secondary: Executive & Export
- **Story 5.4**: Branded Excel Export for client billing.
- **Story 5.1/5.2**: Salary-based Profitability (Sếp only).
